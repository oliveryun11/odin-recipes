Sweet and Sour Pork

Description

Sweet and sour pork, or 糖醋排骨, is a popular dish in Chinese cuisine. There are many different versions of the dish occuring in regional cuisines, including Shandong cuisine, Shanghai cuisine, and Sichuan cuisine, among others.

Ingredients

  • Pork Ribs 700g
  • Cooking Oil 1 Tablespoon
  • Garlic Cloves 10
  • Scallions 2 Stalks
  • White Sugar 5 Tablespoons
  • Rice Vinegar 4 Tablespoons
  • Light Soy Sauce 1 Tablespoon
  • Dark Soy Sauce 1 Tablespoon
  • Salt 1/8 Teaspoon
  • Toasted Sesame Seeds
  • Finely Chopped Scallions

Steps

  1. Boil then simmer the pork ribs in water.
  2. Use a spoon to remove the foam that rises while boiling.
  3. Drain ribs after 3 minutes
  4. Pour cooking oil into clean pan.
  5. Add ribs, garilc, ginger, and scallion.
  6. Stir fry over medium heat until ribs become golden.
  7. Add sugar, 1 teaspoon of rice vineger, dark soy sauce, and salt.
  8. Add hot water until pork ribs are covered.
  9. Bring to a boil and turn heat to low.
  10. After simmering for 50 minutes, remove garlic, ginger, and scallions
  11. Add remaining rice vinegar
  12. Stir occasionally. Once sauce thickens, remove from heat.
  13. Place ribs in a serving bowl, and garnish with sesame seeds and scallions
