Cheesecake

Description

A rich and creamy classic cheesecake over a graham cracker crust.

Ingredients

Crust

  • Graham Cracker Crumps 1.5 Cups
  • White Sugar 2 Tablespoons
  • Brown Sugar 1 Tablespoon
  • Butter 7 Tablespoons

Cheesecake

  • Cream Cheese 32 Ounces
  • White Sugar 1 Cup
  • Sour Cream 2/3 Cups
  • Vanilla Extract 1.5 Teaspoons
  • Salt 1/8 Teaspoon
  • Eggs 4

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160 degrees Celsius
  2. Combine graham cracker crums, sugar, and brown sugar in a bowl. Stir well.
  3. Add butter to bowl and use a fork to mix.
  4. Pour into a pan and press firmly into the bottom and the sides of the pan.
  5. In a bowl, add cream cheese and stir until smooth.
  6. Add sugar and continue stirring until creamy.
  7. Add sour cream, vanilla extract, and salt. Stir until well-combined.
  8. Gradually add lightly beaten eggs, stirring until each egg is incorporated.
  9. Pour cheesecake batter into pan.
  10. Place pan on cookie sheet lined with foil.
  11. Place pan and sheet on center rack of oven.
  12. Bake on 160 degrees Cesius for 60 minutes.
  13. Remove from oven and allow to cool for 10 minutes.
  14. Use a knife to loosen crust from the pan.
  15. Cool for 2 hours at room temperature and transfer to refrigerator to cool for 6 more hours.
